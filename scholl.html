Annual Science Fair Inspires Students

The Lincoln High School Science Fair was held this week, showcasing creative projects from students in all grades. From volcano models to robotics, the event was filled with excitement and innovation.

Interview with the Winner

We spoke with Jane Smith, this year’s first-place winner, about her solar-powered water purifier. “I wanted to find a way to help communities with limited access to clean water,” Jane explained. Her project impressed both judges and visitors.
